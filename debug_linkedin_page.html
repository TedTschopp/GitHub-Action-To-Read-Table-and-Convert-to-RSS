  • Ed Legge

    “For the issue of cyber security – which holds growing importance not only for the electricity industry but for national security – David Batz is the “go-to” expert for electric companies and their trade association, the Edison Electric Institute. I know this because I have worked with David on the front lines of cyber security in our nation’s capital. The great thing about David’s perspective is his combination of experience at the utility-company level, his broad and deep knowledge of the national situation and – perhaps most importantly – his very sane and thoughtful perspective. David takes cyber security very seriously, and he is “dialed in” when it comes to the “ground truth” regarding the security of and potential threats to the electric industry’s computer systems. But in an area where the sky is the limit when it comes to “worst-case-scenario” thinking, David has the extremely valuable ability to step back and put it all in perspective and not give in to the very real and ever-present temptation to overreact. This is why he is not only such an asset to EEI and to the electric industry writ large, but such a trusted and valued colleague and team-member. To use the military metaphor, he’s the kind of person you want by your side if you are going into battle – whether the battle is in cyberspace, the court of public opinion or the boardrooms of the nation’s electric companies.”
